All About Anime

What is anime?

Anime is a term refering to animation that originates from Japan. Most anime are adaptations of different types of manga. These are similar to western comic books; however, it could be argued that anime reaches a broader range of topics and sub-generes.

What should I watch?

When it comes to type of anime there are a few main demographic classifications or target audiences.

  • Shonen: targeted at teen boys, typically action-centered.
  • Shojo: targeted at teen girls, focused on romance, drama, and relationships.
  • Seinen: targeted at adult men, featuring complex themes that can be brutal or more mature.
  • Josei: targeted at adult women, highlighting realisitc relationships and daily life.
  • Kodomomuke: targeted at young children, low stress cultivating friendships.
